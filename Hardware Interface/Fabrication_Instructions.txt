The lines below with "=>" are the only ones that must ne changed in the quote form in PCBWAY.
The defaults will work very well, but I recommend to change "Edge Connector" option to YES - see below.
These instructions will result in the cheapest cost (around USD5 for 5 Units)

=> Length: 64mm, Width: 99.70mm
Layers: 2, Thickness: 1.6mm
Material: FR-4
FR4-TG: TG 150-160
Thickness: 1.6
Min Track/Spacing: 6/6mil
Min Hole Size: 0.3mm
Solder Mask: -> choose the color you like, but some places charge more for this option.
Silkscreen: White (change this color from "white" to something else if you choose "White Solder Mask" above)
=> Edge connector: Yes, Bevelling: Yes - 30 Degrees
=> Surface Finish: HASL lead free (change to "immersion gold" for nice touch, but much more expensive)
